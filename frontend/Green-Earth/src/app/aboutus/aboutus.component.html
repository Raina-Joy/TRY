About Us

Our out reach now spans over 7 districts, delivering sustainable solutions for a lasting Impact

Green Earth is the first and foremost Plastic Waste Management Technology startup dedicated to addressing the pressing plastic waste issues faced by society and the environment as a whole. Our vision is clear: to become the ultimate destination for the disposal of plastic waste, making it easier for everyone to contribute to a cleaner, greener future. Since our inception in 2019, we have consistently led the charge in developing sustainable plastic waste solutions, earning a reputation for our pioneering environmental management, exceptional customer service, and unwavering commitment to sustainability. With a satisfied customer base exceeding 65 thousand, Green Earth stands as a prominent player in Kerala's Plastic waste management sector. Green Earth's core mission revolves around recycling, using a technology-driven platform to optimize plastic waste collection and integrate segregated plastic waste into a vast recycling network. Green Earth's reach extends across municipalities, commercial establishments, and residential communities across Kerala, all managed from the headquarters in Thrissur, Kerala.

OUR MISSION

To provide cutting-edge plastic waste solutions that promote a sustainable, circular economy, reduce environmental impact, and enhance resource efficiency.

OUR VISION

Green Earth's vision is to be a global leader in the transformation of plastic waste management, leading the charge toward a world where plastic waste is no longer a problem but a solution..
