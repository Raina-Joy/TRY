What we do?

GreenEarth is a plastic waste management initiative that came about in 2023. Effective plastic waste collection is a critical component of sustainable waste management systems aimed at mitigating the environmental impact of plastic pollution. It involves the organized gathering and removal of plastic materials from various sources, such as households, businesses, and public spaces, to prevent their improper disposal and eventual entry into ecosystems. This platform is created to collect plastic waste from varous fields such as hoseholds, bussiness and public spaces with the help of technology to ensure zero waste, a greener earth and a sustainable future for all. GreenEarth engages in plastic waste collection, channeling it into end recycling centers to ensure zero waste.
